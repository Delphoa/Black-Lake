The accompanying `.logs` note records the random selection and deduplication pass, while the `.reports` Report-Mark expands the cross-DEP synthesis. This DEP manuscript is the durable research artifact that ties those operational records to reusable evidence, implementation paths, and future review questions.

The bridge is conceptual rather than a claim that the related entries validate 2D-RC. It should guide future source-first reviews around structured channel state, evidence ledgers, privacy-sensitive wireless features, and deployment trust boundaries.
