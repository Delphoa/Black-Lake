## Insights and Relevance

SpecTemp chooses dimension-specific interpolation between PCA and whitening from an unlabeled corpus spectrum. The review finds near-oracle retrieval regret more convincing than the proxy-SNR interpretation, and records a large gamma mismatch, three-run testing weakness, numerical edge cases, and multi-index deployment cost.
