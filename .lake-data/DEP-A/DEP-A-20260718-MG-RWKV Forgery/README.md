# DEP-A-20260718-MG-RWKV Forgery

#artificial-intelligence #temporal-forgery-localization #RWKV #mixture-of-experts #audio-visual #media-integrity

This DEP-A archives a public-safe, whitepaper-grade review of arXiv:2607.00902v1, *MG-RWKV: Multi-Grained Context-Aware RWKV for Temporal Forgery Localization*. The complete PDF and full-paper HTML were verified locally. Source documents, private coverage matrices, cache indexes, validator receipts, and deployment audits remain outside the repository.

## Contents

- `README.md` - classification, deposition context, complete inventory, associations, and source attribution.
- `2607.00902-whitepaper-review.md` - validated full-paper technical reconstruction, experimental and claim audit, independent re-conceptualization, coverage ledger, limitations, and replication agenda.

## Summary of Items

### `README.md`

Defines the archival boundary and gives canonical public provenance without exposing private machine context.

### `2607.00902-whitepaper-review.md`

Reconstructs the paper's mechanism and formal objective, audits the evaluation and reported results, separates paper report from reviewer inference, and records complete section, table, figure, equation, appendix, disclosure, and reproducibility coverage. Its central technical focus is: MG-RWKV uses bidirectional RWKV for linear-time full-sequence context, a multi-granularity mixture of experts with explicit temporal receptive fields and top-k routing, and cross-granularity consistency to suppress boundary false positives. Classification, localization, reconstruction, and consistency losses train the localizer.

## Insights and Relevance

The paper matters because it makes a consequential state, resource, or integrity decision explicit and testable. The review preserves the reported result while retaining its model, workload, metric, and systems boundaries. For Black Lake, the most useful downstream implication is: combine multi-scale routing with calibrated boundary uncertainty and provenance-aware media acquisition so localization confidence and source integrity can be audited together.
