## Summary of Items

### `README.md`

Defines the archival boundary and gives canonical public provenance without exposing private machine context.

### `2604.18260-whitepaper-review.md`

Reconstructs the paper's mechanism and formal objective, audits the evaluation and reported results, separates paper report from reviewer inference, and records complete section, table, figure, equation, appendix, disclosure, and reproducibility coverage. Its central technical focus is: Geo3DPruner projects multi-frame video tokens into a shared geometric representation, groups them by 3D voxels, selects representatives inside each voxel, and enforces inter-voxel spatial diversity before the language model.

## Insights and Relevance

The paper matters because it makes a consequential state, resource, or integrity decision explicit and testable. The review preserves the reported result while retaining its model, workload, metric, and systems boundaries. For Black Lake, the most useful downstream implication is: Attach geometry confidence to every voxel and reserve tokens for depth discontinuities, moving objects, and reconstruction disagreement; evaluate recovery under camera-motion and low-texture stress tests.
