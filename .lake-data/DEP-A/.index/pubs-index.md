# DEP-A Publications Index

This index attributes arXiv papers and other research publications preserved or substantively reviewed by entries in the `DEP-A` container.

Current status: no `DEP-A` entries or indexed publications.

When the first qualifying entry is added, create a section linked to its `README.md` and add a table with these columns:

| Publication | Authors or credited organization | Canonical record |
|---|---|---|
| *Canonical publication title* | *Author names from the canonical record* | *arXiv, DOI, or publisher URL* |

Only index publications actually preserved or reviewed by the DEP. Background citations and unreviewed reading suggestions do not belong here. Keep locally archived source files out of the public repository.
