## Summary of Items

### `2604.10734-whitepaper-review.md`

The review reconstructs semantic grouping, the two-budget multiple-choice knapsack objective, Pareto-pruned dynamic programming, NLI reward, MCTS search, six-dataset evaluation, recall and generation ablations, theoretical appendices, prompts, and failure traces. It explicitly separates evidence consistency from factual truth.

## Insights and Relevance

The paper makes joint evidence-set quality an explicit optimization problem and checks candidate answers against selected evidence during inference. The review recommends source-authority costs, contradiction preservation, claim-level coverage, calibrated abstention, matched-compute baselines, and complete selection provenance for live RAG systems.
