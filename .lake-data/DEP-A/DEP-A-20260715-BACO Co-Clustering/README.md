### `2604.18351-whitepaper-review.md`

This artifact reconstructs BACO's connectivity-and-balance objective, recommendation-aware weighting, label-propagation solver, secondary user codes, sketch matrices, parameter-quality trade-offs, construction speed, subgroup results, cluster diagnostics, and large-scale tests. ## Insights and Relevance

BACO turns embedding collisions into governed graph-derived cohort assignments. For Black Lake, the review shows why a compressed mapping should retain its interaction window, objective parameters, membership hash, compatible checkpoint, structural diagnostics, and refresh history rather than existing as an unexplained index.
