## Summary of Items

The manuscript separates the paper's predictive evidence from its causal interpretation. It records the lag-dependent temporal graph, LLM-based five-score news encoder, functional causal model, six-dataset experiments, ablations, explanation figures, investment simulation, assumptions, and limitations. It also documents uniform random selection, dedup validation, local source repair, and the no-source-upload gate.

## Insights and Relevance

CausalStock is most useful as a design study in explicit evidence routing across news, time, and entity relations. Its results support further replication, especially the large drop when temporal causal discovery is removed, but do not independently establish economic causality or deployable trading value. The closest Black Lake bridges are CausalTAD's assumption-sensitive causal graph, Quantum Quant Trading's end-to-end financial-evidence boundary, and PAC Confidence's calibration and distribution-shift discipline.
