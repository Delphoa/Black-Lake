## Insights and Relevance

CuraLight is most useful as a design study in pairing model-based traffic guidance with curated LLM deliberation. Its reported results support further controlled replication, especially the improvement from imitation to full deliberation and the cross-network Yizhuang evaluation. They do not establish physical-road safety, statistically stable superiority, or faithful explanation. The closest Black Lake bridges are Self Learned IDC's heterogeneous traffic-control representation, OViP Preference's evaluator-driven curation loop, and Agent State Review's public-versus-hidden deliberation boundary.
