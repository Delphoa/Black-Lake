## Insights and Relevance

The bundle's common thread is boundary elevation. AAFLOW+, MemExchange, HCRMap, and GPU-Tile-Sim make hidden runtime structure explicit; the monitoring, AHA, distributional-RL, and ToolAtlas papers make evidence ownership and falsification explicit; NAIS and CatRetriever make governance or physical-parent validity part of the system rather than an afterthought. The practical synthesis is that durable agent state and claims need identity, compatibility or validity predicates, provenance, invalidation rules, and outcome tests. Evidence strength remains uneven: eight full papers were inspected, but MemExchange and CatRetriever were limited to canonical abstracts, several results are simulated or benchmark-bound, and nothing was independently reproduced.
