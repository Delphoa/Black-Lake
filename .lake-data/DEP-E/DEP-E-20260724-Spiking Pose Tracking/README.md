## Insights and Relevance

The paper is a strong Black Lake example of representation-aware efficiency: sparse event streams are kept sparse, binary attention uses a binary-appropriate similarity function, and temporal evidence is fused bidirectionally before structured 3D regression. The three related entries extend that pattern across event-camera association memory, motion-preserving conditioning, and SMPL/geometry priors. Together they motivate an auditable motion stack that reports not only a pose, but also how much each joint depends on current events, temporal context, cross-sensor evidence, and prior-driven inference.
